# TechSuite Corporate Services - Knowledge Base

## Overview
TechSuite provides high-performance computing, cloud storage, and AI-driven analytics to enterprise clients globally.

## Service Level Agreement (SLA)
Our standard SLA guarantees 99.9% uptime for all cloud services. Technical support is available via phone and chat during business hours (9 AM - 6 PM EST).

## Security and Compliance
TechSuite is fully SOC 2 Type II compliant. All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.3.

## Billing and Refunds
Invoices are generated on the 1st of every month. Clients may request a refund for service downtime if the uptime falls below 99.0% in a given month. Standard refunds are processed within 10 business days.

## Data Privacy
We do not share client data with third parties. All data processing occurs within the client's selected geographic region to ensure sovereignty.
